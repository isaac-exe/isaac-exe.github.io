About

Hey I'm Isaac. I recently graduated from Carleton College with a B.A. in Cinema and Media Studies. I'm a budding filmmaker, sometimes writer, and hobbyist programmer.

You can watch my senior thesis project, Let the Good Times Bowl, on Vimeo. It's a short fiction narrative film that's inspired by The Big Lebowski (1998), American 70s Cinema and The French New Wave.

I'm currently looking for jobs in media related fields like publishing, advertising, and production. In my freetime I keep myself busy by writing screenplays, short stories, and music related stuff for Outside Indie. I'm also making a baseball stats web application using a MERN stack and picking up other skills like podcast production, Japanese cooking, and bicycle repair.

Right now I'm reading The Pale King by David Foster Wallace, and have been listening to Slow Pulp, a band from my hometown Madison, WI.
